Strategy

When attacking, raiding or defending your bunker, you need to have some knowledge of how to fight, these following strategies will help you do that.

Attacking and Defending

When infiltrating or raiding houses, buildings, other bunkers, or protecting your base, you should take into account these key points.

  • Scouting - you should always look to recon the area before going into it. Being on the look out for danger, weak points or any other useful information
  • Planning - a vital aspect of the raid, as you need to have a clear plan set in order to be efficent and minimize risks.
  • Organized - you should always be ready and prepared for the mission, a good way to do this is designate people to certian roles based on their skills and experience.
  • Formations - having a formations for your group will drastically effect the chance of injuries and casualites, so it is something you need to take the time learning and practicing.
  • Strengths and Weaknesses - Knowing what you are good at and not is a key part of your teams success in survival, as it could be the differance between life and death. You should always try play to your advantages and know when to fall back or stay put.
Alliances

Alliances could be a good way to boost your resources and strengths, overall increasing your chances of survival. However, in a world where everyone is out for themselves, never fully trust anyone. As deception and sabotage could be the end of it all.
