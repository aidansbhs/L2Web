Food and Water

Food and water is essential for survival, so stocking up is a necessity. However you need to choose items that can be stored easily and last for years.
Here are some recommendations:

  • Honey
  • Uncooked rice
  • Peanut Butter
  • Beans
  • Energy Bars
  • Dehydrated Meat
  • Pasta
  • Canned fruits, vegetables and soups

All of these have great shelf life but you should always store more than you will think you need.
Water should be stored prefferably in jugs or bottles which have been treated to last longer.
